EID-UL-FITR

Eid in Kolkata is celebrated with special prayers at mosques by the
devout and exchange of greetings among family members and friends.
Every year thousands of muslims assembles at the biggest congregation
on the Red Road,Tipu Sultan Mosque in the city to offer special prayers
on the occasion,which marks the end of monthlong Ramzan.Imams of
different mosques calls for peace,harmony and brotherhood while
exchanging wishes and greetings to the people.




HOLI

The festivals of Colours,known as Holi in rest of India,is called
'Dol Yatra' in Bengal.Kolkata also celebrates Holi with fervour.
It is celebrated on full moon day.on this festival,people enjoy the
day by using colours on each other with water and also without
water.Children also use the water gun for playing with colours.
It feels great happiness in the minds of the people to celebrate Holi.
