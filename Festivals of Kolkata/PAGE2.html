FESTIVALS OF KOLKATA

Kolkata (or Calcutta) holds many festivals throughout the year.Durga Puja is the largest festival of West Bengal, and it features colourful pandals, decorative idols of Hindu goddess Durga and her family, lighting decorations and fireworks. Other major festivals are Kali Puja, Holi, Diwali, etc.

DURGA PUJA

The greatest festival of the Bengali community.
Durga Puja ranks among the popular festivals in India.
It is widely celebrated in West Bengal
and other partof eastern India. It is a four day festival
and is dedicated to the worship of Goddess Durga.
Immense fanfare and celebration takes place during this
Durga Puja celebrations.
